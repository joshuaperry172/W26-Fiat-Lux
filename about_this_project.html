About Our Project

This terminal serves as an architectural autopsy of the "Cyberpunk" aesthetic. By isolating sectors of the urban landscape, we analyze the intersection of Brutalist concrete structures and the high-tech neon overlays that define a dystopian future.

Our goal is to provide a digital archive of structural intel, mapping the physical bones of a world where architecture is both a tool of oppression and a canvas for rebellion.
