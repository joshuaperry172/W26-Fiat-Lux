The Freeway: In this still from "Cyberpunk: Edgerunners", we see a crowded freeway leading into Night City. The cars are boxy, with hard edges, and so are the lights and buildings. Cyberpunk here embodies both the futurisitic and the retro; advanced technology merged with anachronistic design principals, an aesthetic choice often fount in Cyberpunk architecture, which tires to imagine a less-than-ideal future through a limited present-tense lens. In the world of Cyberpunk 2077, flying cars exist, and yet the traffic on ordinary highways gets just as bad as it does in Los Angeles. Here we see that the technology of the future mirrors the problems of the present, rather than presenting a solution to them, at least, not a solution that is accessible for the working person. This of course, is quintessential Cyberpunk--high technology, low life.
