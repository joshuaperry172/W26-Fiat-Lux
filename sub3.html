Glowing Descent: Here we see two sets of stairs at nighttime, surrounded by shadow that is held at bay by neon lights. This bluish glow illuminates the stairs--largely a safety concern, partially an aesthetic choice, and another clear example of anachronistic "futuristic" technology. As of the 2026 LED bulbs are the clear winner in terms of power efficiency, although in terms of fictional futurism, where 'efficiency' can always be hand-waved if need be, the best light should be harshly white with a colorful outline. It's not possible to discern exactly what kind of light is being used in the game, of course, since it's not a real place, but it's clear that the aesthetic preference is most aligned with traditional neon lighting, which is even more prominent in downtown areas of night city. This choice makes clear sense from a thematic standpoint--neon lights are eye catching and colorful without, but they aren't so luminous that they eradicate the gloomy shadows and dark alleyways that reflect the moral darkness of the future. In both the real life picture, as well as they cyberpunk frame, the lighting creates a sense of luxary. It's a cool blue, minimalistic and integrated into the enviroment, hallmarks of futurist lighting.
